About Us


Mission

At Relaxing Massage & Wellness, we believe in paving a pathway to wellness, fulfillment and serenity. Our wide variety of services ensures we can meet your every need and at the very least, guarantee you a period of peace and escape from stress. Here at Relaxing, we have a mission to present the highest quality of one-on-one service to our clients; our very experienced Registered Massage Therapists accomplish this by taking the time to learn about their client’s health concerns and creating a safe and tranquil environment.


Vision

Our clinic wishes to go above and beyond client expectations with our great value for quality, associated with our “clients-first” mentality. As we develop, we look forward to increasing our knowledge and skills to better serve our clients and staff in the future.


Value

Clients & Staff – Our clients and staff are our greatest asset; without them, there would be no Relaxing. This is why we make a great effort to treat our clients and staff with integrity that reveals their significance in our ever-growing family.

Respect – We treat our clients and staff equally with the highest degree of respect. We embrace diversity and treat everyone with importance as we are mindful that respect can only be earned when given.

Excellence – Relaxing can guarantee that our best effort will be put into everything we do to ensure we deliver the most effective healing while continually looking for ways to improve.

Consistency – Our clients will consistently receive the highest quality massage and wellness services. Time after time, we will continue to impress our clients as we firmly believe trust is built on consistency.
